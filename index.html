Battleship Game

Battleship-one, a one-player game, where your goal is to destroy all the battleships hidden on a grid. There are 5 battleships - a 5-cell, a 4-cell, 2 3-cell, and one 2-cell battleships. At the beginning of the game, you should upload the dock (send the battleships to the grid) by clicking on each of the battleships. Once the dock is uploaded, the game starts. You should find the battleships by clicking on cells on the grid. If there is a ship placed on a cell, the cell will turn orange, if there is no ship placed on a cell, it will just turn grey color. There is a window with number of turns left for you to keep track of them.
